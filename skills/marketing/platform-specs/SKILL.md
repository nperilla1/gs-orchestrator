# Platform Specifications -- Ad Formats Reference

## Google Ads -- Responsive Search Ads (RSA)
- Headlines: up to 15, max 30 characters each
- Descriptions: up to 4, max 90 characters each
- Display URL paths: 2, max 15 characters each
- Final URL: required
- Pin headlines/descriptions to specific positions if needed

## Google Ads -- Performance Max
- Headlines: up to 5, max 30 characters
- Long headlines: up to 5, max 90 characters
- Descriptions: up to 5, max 90 characters
- Images: landscape (1.91:1, 1200x628), square (1:1, 1200x1200), portrait (4:5, 960x1200)
- Videos: optional, YouTube hosted
- Logos: 1:1 (1200x1200) and 4:1 (1200x300)

## Meta (Facebook/Instagram) -- Feed Ads
- Primary text: 125 characters (before "See more")
- Headline: 40 characters
- Description: 25 characters
- Image: 1080x1080 (1:1) or 1200x628 (1.91:1)
- Video: 1:1 or 4:5, up to 240 minutes, <4GB
- Carousel: 2-10 cards, 1:1 images

## Meta -- Stories/Reels
- Image: 1080x1920 (9:16)
- Video: 9:16, 15-60 seconds for Reels
- Text overlay: keep in safe zone (top/bottom 14% clear)

## LinkedIn -- Sponsored Content
- Introductory text: 150 characters (before truncation on mobile)
- Headline: 70 characters
- Description: 100 characters
- Image: 1200x627 (1.91:1) or 1080x1080 (1:1)
- Video: 16:9 or 1:1, 3 seconds to 30 minutes
- Carousel: 2-10 cards

## LinkedIn -- Message Ads
- Subject: 60 characters
- Body: 1,500 characters
- CTA button: 20 characters

## TikTok -- In-Feed Ads
- Ad description: 1-100 characters (12-34 English chars recommended)
- Video: 9:16, 16:9, or 1:1; 5-60 seconds (9-15s optimal)
- Resolution: 720x1280+ for 9:16
- File size: up to 500MB

## Twitter/X -- Promoted Tweets
- Tweet text: 280 characters
- Image: 1200x675 (16:9) or 1200x1200 (1:1)
- Video: 16:9 or 1:1, up to 2:20
- Website card headline: 70 characters

## Email
- Subject line: 30-50 characters optimal (mobile preview)
- Preheader: 40-130 characters
- Body width: 600px max
- CTA button: 2-5 words
- Images: max 600px wide, include alt text

## YouTube -- Video Ads
- Skippable in-stream: 12 seconds to 3 minutes (15-30s optimal)
- Non-skippable: exactly 15 or 20 seconds
- Bumper: exactly 6 seconds
- Thumbnail: 1280x720
- Title: 100 characters
- Description: 5,000 characters

## Pinterest -- Promoted Pins
- Image: 1000x1500 (2:3) optimal
- Title: 100 characters
- Description: 500 characters

## Reddit -- Promoted Posts
- Headline: 300 characters
- Image: 1200x628 or wider
- Video: 16:9, up to 15 minutes
